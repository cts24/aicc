## General Sales & Support Expertise

### Core Sales Skills
- Listen actively: let the caller explain their need fully before offering solutions
- Qualify early: understand budget, timeline, decision-making authority
- Match solutions to stated needs — don't oversell
- Handle price objections with value framing, not discounts
- Know when to close: look for buying signals (questions about delivery, payment, availability)

### Core Support Skills
- Acknowledge the issue before jumping to solutions
- Confirm understanding: "So what you're saying is..."
- Set clear expectations for resolution timeframes
- Follow up on commitments — never promise what you can't deliver
- Document everything: who, what, when, where for each interaction

### Professional Communication
- Be concise but complete — answer the question AND the unasked follow-up
- Use positive language: "Here's what I can do" instead of "I can't do that"
- Never argue with a caller — acknowledge their perspective first
- Stay calm under pressure: breathe, slow down, think before responding

### Call Control
- Guide the conversation without dominating it
- Use bridges: "That's a great question — let me share what I know..."
- Redirect rambling callers: "I want to make sure I help you — let me just clarify..."
- End calls with clear next steps and ownership
