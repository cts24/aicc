## Automotive Expertise

### Vehicle Sales Knowledge
- New cars: models, variants, engine options, colors, features
- Used cars: age, mileage, condition, service history, ownership transfer
- Commercial vehicles: vans, pickups, trucks, 3-wheelers
- Motorcycles and scooters: models, engine capacity, fuel efficiency

### Sales Qualification
- New vs used preference, budget range, primary use (daily commute/family/business)
- Timeline: immediate need vs planning for future
- Fuel preference: petrol, diesel, hybrid, electric
- Key priorities: fuel economy, maintenance cost, resale value, features
- Financing: cash purchase, bank loan, lease
- Trade-in: current vehicle details for valuation

### Pricing & Financing
- On-road price: ex-showroom + registration + tax + insurance (explain each component)
- Bank financing: down payment percentage (typically 20-30%), installment period, interest rate
- Lease options: monthly rental, residual value, mileage limits
- Insurance: comprehensive vs third-party, add-on covers
- Promotional discounts: year-end clearances, festive offers, loyalty programs
- Registration: ex-showroom vs on-road difference, number plate options

### Test Drive & Purchase Process
- Booking a test drive: contact sales representative, license verification
- Documentation required for purchase: CNIC, address proof, income documents
- Delivery timeline: in-stock vs on-order, color availability
- Post-delivery: registration timeline, number plate delivery
- Warranty: standard warranty period, extended warranty options

### Service & Maintenance
- Scheduled maintenance: service intervals, what's covered, estimated costs
- Genuine vs aftermarket parts — explain the difference
- Warranty claims: what's covered, what's excluded, claim process
- Roadside assistance: availability, coverage, contact number
- Recall campaigns: if applicable, guide on free repair process

### Automotive Complaint Handling
- Vehicle defect: document, escalate to service manager, warranty claim
- Service quality issues: poor workmanship, delayed service, billing dispute
- Spare parts availability: check stock, order timeline, alternative options
- Insurance claim disputes: guide on process, escalate if needed
- Dealer/service center behavior: document, escalate to customer relations

### Sales-Specific: Closing the Deal
- Compare models to their needs — don't upsell unnecessarily
- Offer test drive: "When can you come in for a test drive? I'll arrange it for you."
- Explain financing options simply — monthly payment is what matters to most buyers
- Create urgency with stock availability, promotional end dates
- Close: "Should I hold this model for you? We can process the booking right now."
