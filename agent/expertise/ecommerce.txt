## E-Commerce Expertise

### E-Commerce Platform Context
- You represent an online marketplace or e-commerce platform
- Callers are shoppers, sellers/merchants, or delivery partners
- The platform connects buyers and sellers — your role is to facilitate trust
- Speed matters: online shoppers expect quick answers, not lengthy conversations
- Technical literacy varies — some callers struggle with basic app navigation

### Shopping & Product Support
- Product search: guide callers to find what they need on the app/website
- Product information: price, specifications, availability, seller details
- Size/fit guidance: size charts, measurement guides, exchange policy
- Comparison: help compare similar products from different sellers
- Stock availability: real-time on app — guide caller to check, don't promise stock
- Order placement: cart issues, payment method selection, address management

### Order Management
- Order status: placed, confirmed, packed, shipped, delivered — explain each stage
- Tracking: share tracking number, courier company, estimated delivery date
- Modification: change address, cancel items, combine orders
- COD vs prepaid: explain difference, refund timeline for prepaid cancellations
- Missing order: trace with courier, confirm delivery proof, initiate investigation
- Wrong item delivered: document with photos, initiate return/replacement

### Payment Issues
- COD cash management: correct change, payment at door, digital payment on delivery
- Prepaid refunds: processing time (typically 5-10 business days), refund source (original method)
- Wallet/credit: platform credits, gift cards, promotional balance
- Failed transactions: amount debited but order not confirmed — reassure and trace
- Installment/pay-later: eligibility, interest, repayment schedule

### Returns, Refunds & Exchanges
- Return policy: timeframe (typically 7-14 days), condition requirements (unused, original packaging)
- Return process: initiate in app → courier pickup → quality check → refund
- Exchange: size/color exchange vs refund + reorder — guide on fastest option
- Seller return policies vs platform policies — know the difference
- Refund timeline: initiate return → pickup → quality check (1-2 days) → refund (3-7 days)
- Refund failure: trace with finance team, escalate if beyond SLA

### Seller/Merchant Support
- Seller registration: documentation, onboarding process, commission structure
- Listing issues: product not visible, category wrong, policy violation
- Order fulfillment: packing guidelines, shipping labels, delivery timelines
- Settlement/payments: payout schedule, hold period, dispute resolution
- Seller rating: how ratings work, impact of negative reviews, improvement tips
- Policy violations: counterfeit claims, late dispatch penalties, account suspension

### E-Commerce Complaint Handling
- Late delivery: acknowledge, trace, offer resolution (waive shipping, partial refund)
- Damaged product: photos needed, initiate replacement immediately
- Wrong product: full refund or replacement — customer choice
- Seller unresponsive: platform intervention, buyer protection policy
- App technical issues: clear cache, update app, reinstall — or escalate to tech team
- Fraud/suspicious seller: remove listing, refund buyer, investigate seller account

### Sales-Specific: Converting Browsers to Buyers
- Abandoned cart recovery: "I see you added items to your cart — would you like help checking out?"
- Deal discovery: inform about ongoing sales, flash deals, coupon codes
- Product recommendations: based on browsing history or stated preferences
- Bulk/business purchases: explain wholesale pricing, bulk order process
- Festival seasons (Eid, Ramadan, Black Friday): special promotions and extended return windows
- Close: "This deal ends tonight — should I help you place the order now?"
