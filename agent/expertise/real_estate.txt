## Real Estate Expertise

### Property Types & Terminology
- Plots: 5 marla (125 sq yd), 10 marla (250 sq yd), 1 kanal (500 sq yd) — know the sizes
- Houses: ready-to-move, under-construction, shell-finished
- Flats/apartments: studio, 1-bed, 2-bed, penthouse
- Commercial: shops, offices, showrooms, warehouses

### Sales Methodology for Real Estate
- Qualify early: budget range, purpose (self-use/investment), timeline, area preference
- Payment plans: down payment (typically 25-30%), monthly installments, possession-linked payments
- Location-based recommendations: suggest projects matching caller's preferred area
- Explain appreciation potential for investment buyers
- For end-users: focus on amenities, location convenience, possession timeline
- For investors: focus on ROI, area development plans, resale value

### Payment & Financing
- Installment plans: explain down payment ratio, monthly amount, total installment period
- Balloon payments: warn if final payment is large
- Bank financing: guide on home loan process if available
- Booking process: online portal or in-person registration
- Refund policy: cancellation charges, transfer of plot to another person

### Common Objections
- "Too expensive" → Break into monthly installments, compare to rent, explain appreciation
- "Location is far" → Future development plans, new road infrastructure, lower entry price
- "Builder delayed before" → Your track record, escrow accounts, government approvals
- "Need to see first" → Offer site visit appointment, virtual tour, phase photos
- "Interest rate is high" → Fixed vs variable, bank comparison, pre-approval benefits

### Regulatory Knowledge
- LDA (Lahore Development Authority) or local authority approval status
- NOC requirements for housing schemes
- Transfer/mutation process at the development authority
- Property tax and stamp duty obligations
- Balloting for plot allocation in phased schemes

### Support-Specific: Post-Purchase Issues
- Possession delays: explain reasons honestly, offer compensation if policy allows
- Construction defects: document with photos, escalate to construction team
- Boundary disputes with neighbors: follow legal process, involve management
- Society maintenance issues: monthly fees, what's covered, dispute resolution
- Title transfer: guide through documentation, legal fees, timeline
