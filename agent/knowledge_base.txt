ORGANIZATION OVERVIEW
---------------------
Official Name: Punjab Sahulaat Bazaars Authority (PSBA)
Website: psba.gop.pk
Former Name: Punjab Model Bazaars Management Company (PMBMC)
Supervising Department: Food Safety & Consumer Protection Department, Government of Punjab
Legal Status: Statutory public authority under The Punjab Sahulat Bazaars Authority Act 2025
Nature: Pakistan's first subsidy-free, self-sustaining public welfare authority of its kind
Established: 2011 (Punjab Model Bazaars initiative); PSBA formally notified April 2025
Mission: Operate regulated bazaars across Punjab providing essential commodities at government-notified (DC) rates in a clean, respectful environment.
Certifications: ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management) and ISO 27001:2022 (Information Security) — awarded December 2025

KEY SERVICES
------------
1. PERMANENT SAHULAT BAZAARS — Fixed physical locations across all 36 districts of Punjab; year-round operation; essential commodities at DC-notified rates (approx. 35% below open market). 46+ permanent bazaars, 35+ cities.
2. MOBILE BAZAARS ("Sahulat on the Go") — 60 organized stalls per mobile unit at strategic hotspots; 9 AM–10 PM; expanding across Punjab.
3. HOME DELIVERY APP — "Sahulat Bazaar" app; free home delivery; cash on delivery only; 35 cities; launched January 2026; 312,000+ orders delivered by March 2026.
4. STALL ALLOTMENT — Vendor/entrepreneur stall rental via open electronic balloting; Rs 8,000–15,000/month all-inclusive; 50% discount for women entrepreneurs; 20% stalls reserved for women.
5. RAMAZAN BAZAARS — 74 bazaars operating province-wide during Ramazan; extended hours before Iftar; additional discounts on select items.

PRODUCTS & PRICING
------------------
Core products: Potatoes, onions, tomatoes, pumpkin, garlic, lemon, apples, bananas, dates, guava, watermelon, chickpeas, gram flour (besan), wheat flour, sugar.
Additional items (select locations): Chicken, eggs, meat, rice, pulses, cooking oil, garments, seasonal fruits and vegetables.
Pricing policy:
- Set approx 7% below DC (District Commissioner) officially notified rates
- Approx 35% below open market prices
- Prices updated DAILY — displayed on price boards at each bazaar and on psba.gop.pk/daily-rate-comparison
- Total consumer savings to date: ~Rs 110 million (March 2026)
- PSBA is fully self-sustaining — no operational government subsidies
- Cash only — digital payments NOT yet available at bazaars or on app

HOME DELIVERY APP
-----------------
App Name: Sahulat Bazaar
Developer/Publisher: Punjab Model Bazaars Management Company (PSBA)
Available on: Google Play Store and Apple App Store — search "Sahulat Bazaar"
Launched: January 16, 2026
Features: Browse 13+ core essential items at DC-notified prices, free home delivery, cash on delivery (COD), order tracking, report/complaint function
Coverage: 35 cities via 46+ Sahulat Bazaars
Delivery: Free — zero delivery charges. Cash on delivery only.
Registration: Download app → register with phone number and delivery address → confirm city eligibility → browse items → place order → pay cash on delivery
IMPORTANT: PSBA NEVER asks for advance fees, registration payments, or bank transfers before delivery — any such request is FRAUD.

HOW TO SHOP IN PERSON
----------------------
1. Locate nearest bazaar (via Sahulat Bazaar app or psba.gop.pk)
2. Bring cash — digital payments not yet available
3. No membership, ID card, or registration required — open to all
4. Confirm price against displayed rate board before purchasing
5. Request receipt before leaving
6. Report any overcharging to: complaint desk at bazaar OR call 03070002345

STALL HOLDER / VENDOR INFORMATION
----------------------------------
Eligibility: Open to general public; no specific income criteria; women entrepreneurs especially encouraged
Allotment process: Open electronic balloting system (transparent, merit-based); 400+ stalls allotted in initial phases; one-year renewable leases
Stall rent options:
- Standard: Rs 8,000–15,000/month (all-inclusive: electricity, security, cleanliness, parking, utilities)
- Token rent: Rs 6,000/month (covers utilities, security, sanitation, maintenance)
- Women entrepreneurs: 50% discount on stall rent
- Minimum 20% stalls reserved for women (currently 22% participation)
Facilities provided free to stall holders: Electricity, security (guards + CCTV), sanitation, prayer area, washrooms, parking

CUSTOMER FACILITIES AT BAZAARS
--------------------------------
Free parking, shopping trolleys/carts, food courts/cafeterias, children's play areas (Joyland at select locations 3PM–11PM), prayer facilities, washrooms, complaint resolution desk, CCTV surveillance, covered/shaded shopping environment, daily price boards

COMPLAINTS & RETURNS
---------------------
How to file a complaint (4 methods):
1. Mobile App: Orders → Report Issue → categorize → attach photos
2. Phone: 042-99001000 extension 666
3. Email: establishment@psba.gop.pk (include order ID, photos, description)
4. In-store: Visit local Sahulat Bazaar complaint resolution desk
5. CM Complaint Cell: 0800-02345 (24/7, toll-free, nationwide)
What qualifies: Overcharging, damaged goods, incorrect delivery, missing items, vendor misconduct
Returns: Inspect at purchase; quality issues replaced at bazaar; for app orders, refuse damaged delivery at handover
Resolution timeline: 1–3 days initial review; up to 14 days for refund
